
Recommended Architecture: Lambda Architecture
For a predictive maintenance system, the Lambda Architecture is the most appropriate choice.

Predictive maintenance fundamentally requires two distinct data processing capabilities:

Deep Historical Analysis: The ability to store and analyze massive amounts of historical sensor data to train complex machine learning models capable of predicting wear and tear.

Real-Time Inference: The ability to process continuous, high-velocity streams of live sensor data (like temperature, vibration, or RPMs) to catch immediate anomalies before a machine physically breaks down.
